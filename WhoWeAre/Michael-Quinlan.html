Michael Quinlan, CPA

Senior Vice President Fund Accounting and Finance

Email

Mr. Quinlan is a senior vice president of fund accounting and finance with leadership responsibility for real estate accounting, investor reporting and cash management.

Mr. Quinlan has more than 27 years of hospitality industry experience specializing in real estate accounting and finance. Previously, he held senior leadership roles with four publicly traded real estate investment trusts.

Mr. Quinlan received a B.S. in Accounting from the University of New Orleans.
