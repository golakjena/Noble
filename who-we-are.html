The Noble Organization

Founded in 1993, Noble has specialized in making value-added and opportunistic investments in the lodging and hospitality real estate sector. Through our private equity real estate funds, Noble has made more than $3 billion of investments in primarily upscale select service and extended-stay hotels throughout the United States.

Over more than 20 years, Noble has purposefully built a best-in-class team of lodging specialists who have broad expertise in real estate and capital markets to identify accretive investment opportunities, complex re-development acumen to maximize the income production of every square foot of lodging real estate, revenue and operating strategies to drive daily and longer-term financial performance, and an institutional investment management team with a strong track record as a fiduciary. Noble’s integrated team of principals invest alongside each Noble platform, working closely together as partners to execute a hands-on strategy and then sharing in the financial success of the organizations collective results.

Noble has a deep rooted commitment to maintaining an active role in our communities where we remain personally and financially engaged to make the biggest impact on the cities in which we live, work and play. Through the design, development and re-development of our assets, Noble ensures minimal impact on ecosystems, reduces potable water consumption, promotes better building energy performance and participates in ongoing recycling and energy reduction initiatives. Noble pioneered the development of one of the first LEED (Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design) certified hotels in the United States and the very first in the state of Georgia. Education remains Noble’s cornerstone civic focus. Spanning three decades, Noble and its leaders have provided mentorships, scholarships and workforce preparedness in order to enrich student education at both the high school and collegiate level.

The combination of our deep-rooted culture, our structure and our focused experience has allowed the Noble organization to have access to a broader spectrum of opportunities not readily available to the open market. This advantage, along with Noble’s alignment of interests, core values and performance-based framework, has enabled the organization to realize an exceptional realized investment track record over multiple market cycles.
